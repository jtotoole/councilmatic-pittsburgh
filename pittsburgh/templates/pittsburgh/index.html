Welcome! Pittsburgh Councilmatic is a tool to help residents engage with city government. You can search legislation, register to speak at a hearing, or create personalized alerts for a subject or specific bill.

Pittsburgh City Council consists of nine elected council members who draft legislation to shape the city. The council works with Bill Peduto, the Mayor, and Brenda Pree, the City Clerk.

Pittsburgh Councilmatic tracks all things related to Pittsburgh City Council:

  • the legislation introduced and passed
  • its various committees and the meetings they hold
  • the bill sponsorships and voting records of council members
